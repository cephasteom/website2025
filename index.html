Cephas Teom | music, research, software

About

Dr. Peter Thomas is an artist and researcher exploring algorithmic music, unconventional computing and web technologies. He develops open-source systems for sound design, composition, and performance, with an emphasis on spontaneity and improvisation. He performs and releases music under the pseudonym Cephas Teom, and is a founding member of cult post-folk ensemble The Nightjar.
