About

Dr. Peter Thomas is an artist and researcher exploring algorithmic music, unconventional computing and web technologies. He develops open-source systems for sound design, composition, and performance, with an emphasis on spontaneity and improvisation. He performs and releases music under the pseudonym Cephas Teom, and is a co-founder of software lab Lunar.

Projects

  • Zen | Browser-based live coding language and environment for quantum computer music
  • Actias | Networkable, quantum computer musical instrument, built for Moth Quantum
  • Q1 Synth | Prototype of quantum-inspired synthesizer, devised by Eduardo Miranda, built by Peter Thomas
  • Automaton | Generative piece based on multiple bouncing balls
  • zmod | Live coding language for modular synthesis in the browser
  • ct-synths | Suite of soft synths, sampling instruments, and audio effects, compiled to WASM for browsers
